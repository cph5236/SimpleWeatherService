# Privacy Policy

**Simple Weather Service**
_Last updated: March 16, 2026_

---

## Overview

Simple Weather Service is a straightforward weather app. This policy explains what data is involved when you use it.

---

## Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, transmit, or sell any personal data. Specifically:

- We do not store your location or search history
- We do not create user accounts or profiles
- We do not use advertising networks or tracking pixels
- We do not sell, share, or monetize any data about you
- We do not use cookies for tracking or analytics

---

## Location Data

If you use the "Use Current Location" feature, your device's GPS coordinates are used **only** to fetch weather data for your area. This happens entirely in your browser — coordinates are sent directly to the Open-Meteo weather API and are never stored by us.

If you search by city name, that query is sent directly to the Open-Meteo geocoding API. We do not log or retain these queries.

---

## Third-Party Services

Weather data is provided by [Open-Meteo](https://open-meteo.com), an open-source weather API. When the app fetches weather data, your request goes directly from your browser to Open-Meteo's servers. Please refer to [Open-Meteo's privacy policy](https://open-meteo.com/en/terms) for details on how they handle API requests.

---

## Local Storage

The app may use your browser's local storage to remember preferences such as your unit selection (°F / °C). This data never leaves your device.

---

## Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the "Last updated" date at the top will be revised. Continued use of the app after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

---

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on the project's GitHub repository.
